# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Standards

- Be respectful and constructive
- Welcome diverse viewpoints
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the research community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
